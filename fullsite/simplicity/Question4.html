Question 4 - Circuit Theory

Electrcal circuits are often treated as black boxes. Here you are provided with one such black box. You are required to determine the least number of elements required to realise such a black box, given the transfer function of the block in the Laplace domain.

For example, if the transfer function of the circuit was given by $H(s) = \frac{1}{1 + s}$, then we can realise the circuit as a simple combination of a capacitor in parallel with a shunt resistance. It can also be realised as an inductor in series with a resistance. Either way circuit realised requires $2$ componets atleast to synthesise.



Determine the number of componets required to synthesise another polynomial of the form $\frac{a_ms^m + a_{m-1}s^{m-1} + ... + a_1s + a_0}{b_ns^n + b_{n-1}s^{n-1} + ... + b_1s + b_0}$.
Given a csv file with the coefficients of the transfer function, find the minimum number of elements required to synthesise the circuit. Mail the answer to simplicity.tronix15@gmail.com.
